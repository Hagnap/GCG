    Your beverages, golf balls and tees all have a place in your golf carts dashboard...so why doesn't your hot dog. The Dog'on Caddy is the first ever hands-free hot dog holder for your golf cart. It allows golfers to enjoy their treasured clubhouse snack rather than throwing it back in anticipation of their next shot. Eating your hot dog on the golf course shouldn't be a race, so enjoy it at your own pace!

    The Dog'on Caddy also includes a complimentary mesh travel bag that allows for easy accessibility and storage.
